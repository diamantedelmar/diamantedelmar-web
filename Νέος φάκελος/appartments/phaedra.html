Phaedra

An elegant and romantic retreat for two.

Light and Refined Beauty

Named for the Cretan princess of myth, Phaedra embodies a light and refined beauty perfect for a romantic escape. The space is dominated by elegant gray tones, creating a serene and sophisticated atmosphere that invites you to unwind. Natural light pours in, highlighting the modern furnishings and thoughtful details that define this exclusive retreat.

Designed strictly for adults, Phaedra offers an intimate and tranquil setting. Whether you are enjoying a morning coffee on the balcony with a glimpse of the sea or relaxing in the plush queen-sized bed, every moment here is crafted for connection and peace. It's the ideal backdrop for creating cherished memories together.
