Rhodes

A spacious apartment defined by modern sophistication.

Understated Modern Luxury

Named for the majestic island of the Knights, the Rhodes apartment exudes an air of quiet confidence and modern sophistication. Its generous 40m² floor plan is masterfully appointed with a palette of warm, deep grays and contemporary furnishings. This creates a calm, grounding atmosphere that is both luxurious and eminently comfortable, perfect for the discerning traveler.

The apartment provides an elevated experience for small groups or families, with a cozy full bed and a sleek sofa bed offering flexible sleeping arrangements. Every element within Rhodes has been chosen to contribute to a seamless and stylish stay. It is a space designed not just for rest, but for refined living, providing a sophisticated home base from which to explore the wonders of Kos.
