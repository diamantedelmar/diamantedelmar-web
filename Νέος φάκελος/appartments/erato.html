Erato

Generously spacious and flooded with natural light.

A Sanctuary of Space and Light

Named after the muse of love poetry, Erato is our most generously proportioned apartment. At 40 square meters, it is a true sanctuary of space, designed for those who appreciate room to breathe, relax, and live. The apartment is bathed in an abundance of natural light, which enhances the clean lines, modern decor, and airy, open-plan layout.

This luminous space is ideal for families or groups of friends who desire both comfort and style. The layout includes a plush queen bed and a comfortable sofa bed, providing flexible sleeping arrangements without sacrificing living space. Erato offers a superior level of comfort, making it the perfect choice for a longer stay or for those who simply wish to indulge in a more expansive living experience during their Kos holiday.
