Crete

Immerse yourself in a chic, sea-inspired atmosphere.

An Aegean Dream

Step into the Crete apartment and be transported to a chic, nautical world. Inspired by the deep blue of the Aegean and the vibrant atmosphere of a seaside taverna, this space is defined by its striking color palette and artistic details. Deep sea-blue accents contrast beautifully with crisp whites and natural wood tones, creating an environment that is both invigorating and relaxing.

This spacious 40m² apartment offers a unique and memorable stay for families or friends. The thoughtful layout includes a comfortable full-sized bed and a separate sofa bed, ensuring ample space for everyone. The 'seafood atmosphere' is a tribute to the island's culinary heritage, making Crete a perfect choice for guests who appreciate character, style, and a strong sense of place.
