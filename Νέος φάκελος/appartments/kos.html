Kos

A welcoming, sunlit space that feels like home.

The Heart of Hospitality

Named for our beloved home island, the Kos apartment is the heart of our collection, embodying the spirit of warm Greek hospitality. This sunlit space is designed to feel like a home away from home, with its inviting atmosphere, comfortable furnishings, and warm, sandy beige color scheme accented with touches of gold. It is a space designed for easy living and pure relaxation.

Perfectly suited for any traveler—couples, friends, or small families—the Kos apartment offers a versatile and welcoming environment. The smart layout includes a comfortable queen bed and a practical sofa bed, ensuring a restful night's sleep for up to three guests. Here, you can truly settle in and enjoy the simple pleasures of island life, all within a beautifully appointed, modern setting.
