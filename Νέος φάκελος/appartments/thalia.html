Thalia

A cozy and comfortable haven for small families or friends.

Comfort and Joy

Named after the muse of comedy and idyllic poetry, Thalia is a space that radiates comfort and joy. It is the perfect, flexible accommodation for a small family or a trio of friends looking for a charming base to explore the island of Kos. The design is bright and welcoming, with warm neutral tones that create a relaxing and homey atmosphere from the moment you step inside.

The apartment is efficiently laid out to maximize comfort, featuring a comfortable queen bed and a versatile sofa bed in the living area. The private balcony offers a pleasant view of the city and our garden, providing a quiet spot to enjoy your morning coffee or an evening drink. Thalia is a place where simplicity meets comfort, ensuring a delightful and stress-free stay for all its guests.
