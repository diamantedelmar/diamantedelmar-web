The Perfect Location

Nestled in the desirable Lambi Beach area, Diamante Del Mar is your serene base for island exploration. You are just a 2-minute walk from the organized sandy shores of Lambi Beach and a pleasant 10-minute stroll from the vibrant heart of Kos Town.

Enjoy the best of both worlds: the tranquility of a residential street and immediate access to the island's finest restaurants, cafes, and shops. The ancient Agora, the port, and other key archaeological sites are all within easy reach, inviting you to discover the rich history of Kos at your own pace.
